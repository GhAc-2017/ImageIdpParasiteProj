Ehrlich’s Haematoxylin and Eosin

A good general stain

Method
    1. Bring sections down to water. Iodine treatment is necessary for Zenker fixed tissue.
    2. Stain with Ehrlich’s haematoxylin for 30 minutes.
    3. Wash in water and differentiate with 1% acid alcohol, for a few seconds.
    4. Blue well in running tap water.
    5. Stain with 0.5% aqueous eosin, 10 minutes after formalin fixation, a few seconds after Zenker fixation.
    6. Differentiate in running tap water
    7. Dehydrate, clear and mount in D.P.X.

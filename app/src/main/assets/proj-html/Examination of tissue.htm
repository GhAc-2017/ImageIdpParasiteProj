Examination of Tissues for Parasites

Organ or Tissue Smears :-

1.    The organ or tissue to be examined  is removed from the body.

2.    A portion is cut off with a sharp knife, care being taken not to squeeze the tissue         more than necessary.

3.    The cut surface is drawn along a clean slide.

4.    A series of these preparations is made.

5.    The smear are dried in the air.

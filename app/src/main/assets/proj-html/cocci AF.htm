AF-AO Method

Method
    1. Bring sections down to water.
    2. Stain in Ehrlich’s haematoxylin for 10 minutes. Wash in water, differentiate in acid alcohol, blue in lithium carbonate for a few seconds, wash well in water.
    3. Stain in 1% aqueous acid fuchsin for 5 minutes.
    4. Wash in running water for 1 minute.
    5. Stain in aniline blue- Orange G mixture for 30 seconds.
    6. Wash in running water to remove excess stain, 30-40 seconds.
    7. Dehydrate in two changes of isopropyl alcohol, clear in xylene and mount in D.P.X.

Aniline blue – Orange G solution

Anilin blue-----------------0.5 g

Orange G ------------------1.0 g

Glacial acetic acid --------8 ml

Distilled water ------------100 ml

Heat the days in distilled water, cool, filter and add acetic acid.

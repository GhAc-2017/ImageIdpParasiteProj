Preputial sampling for Trichomoniasis tests

Equipment

50 ml syringe.

Pipette – plastic insemination sheath (Split end, cut off about 4 cm).

25 ml sterile buffered saline – pH 7.2.

Method :

            1.   Take the warm solution into syringe and attach plastic pippete.

            2.   Introduce the pippete into the preputial cavity to a distance of about 30 cm.

            3.    The assistant now holds the syringe while the operator seals the preputial orifice with the fingers of one hand.

            4.    Inject the saline into the preputial cavity, detaches the syringe and closes the free end of the pipette by finger                                                                 pressure.

                5.     Massage the fluide within the cavity with free hand. At least ten vigorous massage movements should be                          carried out.

                6.     Puts the free end of the pippete into a bottle , while re-opening the close end by squeezing.

                7.     Gradually withdraws the pippete, allowing the flow into the bottle. The flow will continue as the pippete                          is withdrown deliberately and progressively. The fluid return can be massaging towards the preputial                          orifice.

The method requires the recovery of 20-25 ml of fluid for laboratory test. The pippetes are disposable and must not be re-used. In the event of failure to recover 20 ml of irrigating fluid, the procedure should be repeated.

Regaud’s Iron Haematoxylin

This method gives improved nuclear differentiation and is useful for photography.

Method
    1. Bring sections down to water and mordant in fresh 5% iron alum for 24 hours.
    2. Stain for 24 hours in haematoxylin solution.
    3. Rinse in water, clear, and differentiate in alcoholic picric acid, wash throughly in water.
    4. Dehydrate, clear and mount in D.P.X

Stain 10% solution of alcoholic haematoxylin

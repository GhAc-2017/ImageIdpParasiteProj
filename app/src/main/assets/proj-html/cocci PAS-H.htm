PAS-H

Method
    1. Bring sections down to water.
    2. Treat for 10 minutes in 0.5% periodic acid and wash well in water.
    3. Staine for 10 minutes in schiff’s reagent.
    4. Wash in water for 10 minutes.
    5. Stain in Harris’s haematoxylin for 30 seconds.
    6. Wash in water for 1 minute.
    7. Dehydrate, clear and mount in D.P.X

Preparation of basic fuchsin-Schiff
    1. Dissolve 0.5 g basic fuchsin by pouring over it 100 ml boiling distilled water. Shake throughly, cool to 500C and filter.
    1. Add 10 ml N.HCL and 1 g potassium metabisulphite and shake. Close and store in dark for 24 hours.
    2. Add 0.25 g neutral activated charcoal, shake for 1 minute and filter, store in dark bottle in the refrigerator.

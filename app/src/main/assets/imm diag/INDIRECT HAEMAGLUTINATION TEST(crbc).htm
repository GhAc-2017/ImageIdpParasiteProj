INDIRECT HAEMAGLUTINATION TEST

(USING CHICK RBC)

Test Animation

Reagents

    • Phosphate buffer saline (PBS)

0.15M KH2PO4 and 0.15M Na2HPO4 solutions were mixed in suitable propotions to give the required pH 7.2 and 6.4. After attaining the required pH, one volume of this buffer was added to 9 volume of normal saline sloution ( 0.85% NaCl ).

    • Tannic acid

Fresh solution of tannic acid was prepared by dissolving 100 mg of reagent grade tannic acid in 100 ml of PBS (pH 7.2). The dilution of tannic acid in solution was 1:1000. This was the stock solution. The stock solution was diluted 1:40000 for tanning the red blood cells throughout the experiment.

    • Chick blood

Blood from 4-6 weeks old chickens were collected in sterile 3.8% trisodium citrate at a 1:4 ratio and the cells were stored at 40C for atleast 2 days to age.

    • Antigens

Antigens at a concentration of 0.5 mg/ml in the phosphate buffer saline (pH 6.4).

    • Rabbit serum

Blood was collected from a healthy rabbit. The serum was separated and inactivated at 600C for 20 minutes.

One ml. Of serum was mixed with 99 ml of phosphate buffer saline (pH 7.2). The rabbit serum was tested to ensure that it did not react on its own with tanned chick RBC. Formaldehyade solution was used for stabilisation of chick RBC.

    • Formaldehyde solution (40%) for stabilisation of chick RBC.
